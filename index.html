CLAIRIgene

The Advanced Way

Welcome to CLAIRIgene, the advanced biotechnology startup paving the way to the development and manufacturing of viral vector platforms for translational research. We focus on achieving the most efficient development of viral products paired with genome-editing technologies aiming to address unmet needs in CNS diseases. We are a team of passionate scientists who all share a vision for success.

Our company focuses on the development of viral vector platforms for the modeling and treatment of neurodegenerative diseases with the use of genome and epigenome-editing technologies. We have more than 15 years of experience in developing lentiviral, retroviral and adeno-associated vector platforms for gene therapy applications.

Our Technology

CLAIRIgene bases its research and development on proprietary viral platforms for modeling and treatment of neurogenerative syndromes and disorders. Our models are applied in animals to generate disease-specific phenotypes and develop treatment regimens. We pair next-generation of viral vectors with innovative genome editing tools to develop sophisticated molecular tools for safe and efficient delivery and expression of gene-therapy products targeting CNS diseases and conditions.
